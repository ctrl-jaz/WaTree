About us:

We make it easier for you to find trees that need water, water them,
and leave a positive impact on your enviroment.

How we started:

Our founder, wanted to help grow and take care of trees. She identifies the main issue
is that people, dont know where to start when it comes to taking care of trees. So she created
an app to help people keep track and find trees to water and places that need trees to be planted.
